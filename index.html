✨ Features

Everything you need to become fluent

From AI-powered conversations to spaced repetition, LinguaBot gives you every tool to master a new language.

🤖

AI Conversation Partner

Practice real conversations with an AI tutor that adapts to your level, corrects mistakes in real-time, and speaks back to you naturally.

🧠

Adaptive Learning Engine

Our spaced-repetition algorithm tracks every word you know and schedules reviews at the perfect moment for maximum retention.

🎮

Gamified Progress

Earn XP, maintain daily streaks, unlock achievements, and compete with friends on global leaderboards to keep you motivated.

🎙️

Pronunciation Coach

Advanced speech recognition scores your pronunciation and provides instant phonetic feedback with native speaker comparisons.

📚

Curated Story Lessons

Learn vocabulary and grammar in context through engaging stories written by native speakers across 50+ languages.

📵

Fully Offline Mode

Download entire language packs and learn anywhere without internet. All core features work offline, always.

⚙️ Process

Start speaking in days, not years

1

Pick Your Language & Goal

Choose from 50+ languages and set your goal — travel, business, or full fluency. Our AI builds your personalised curriculum.

Practice with the AI Tutor

Have real conversations on any topic. The AI corrects your mistakes gently and teaches you naturally.

4

Track Your Fluency Progress

Watch your fluency score rise weekly. Get detailed reports on vocabulary, grammar, listening, and speaking.

🏢 About Us

Building the future of language learning

LinguaBot was founded in 2022 by a team of linguists, AI researchers, and passionate polyglots. We were frustrated with language apps that relied on repetitive drills and gamification tricks without actual conversational practice.

Our mission is simple: make fluency achievable for everyone. We believe technology — specifically AI — can be the patient, always-available language tutor that most people never had access to.

Today, we're a fully open-source project hosted on GitHub, driven by a global community of contributors who share our belief that language learning should be free, effective, and actually fun.
